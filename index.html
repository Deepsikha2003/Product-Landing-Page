– Our Story –

Rooted in Passion

In the lush, emerald stretches of India’s Western Ghats and Eastern highlands, where the morning mist kisses centuries-old plantations, our journey was brewed — not in a boardroom, but in the heart of a true coffee romantic. Born from a personal devotion to the ritual of coffee, this endeavor is an ode to the richness of Indian soil, the hands that nurture it, and the timeless heritage it carries. From the bold, earthy beans of Chikmagalur and Coorg to the nuanced, floral notes of the Araku Valley, we source our coffee exclusively from India’s finest estates — handpicked, sun-dried, and roasted to perfection.

Every blend we offer is an embodiment of craftsmanship and care — a seamless fusion of tradition, sustainability, and an unrelenting passion for the perfect cup. We work hand-in-hand with local farmers, embracing ethical sourcing and honoring age-old techniques, ensuring that every sip tells a story of provenance and pride. At the heart of our brand lies a simple belief: coffee isn’t just a beverage — it’s a legacy, a love, and a lifestyle. And through every pour, we invite you to experience India in its most aromatic form.
